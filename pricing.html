Pricing

We offer our users and clients a wide range of choices when it comes to pricing. We believe that everyone should have the right to learn using the latest technologies, thus we are committed to helping students access our platform with reasonable prices.
Basic Plan

For free you get access to the courses and quizzes offered by your university using your university assigned email. You can compete with other university students for the top ranks in the leaderboards and earn benefits and badges.

Pro Plan

For $10/month you get access to a larger community of highly motivated students from all over the country as well as the national leaderboard. You also get access to the community portal where you can team up with other pro members and work on together to solve the monthly tasks and earn valuable prizes.

Elite Plan

For $20/month you unlock access to our full feature set including the most interesting global leaderboard as well as access to courses and quizzes from universities from all around the world. You get the chance to connect with students from other countries and make limitless friendships with us.
